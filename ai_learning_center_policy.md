# AI Learning Center — Policy Document

## Refunds & Cancellations
- Full refunds are available within **14 days** of purchase.
- Refunds are not available after a course has been **completed**.
- If a course is **partially completed**, refunds may be prorated at the organization’s discretion.
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method within **5–10 business days** after approval.
- Subscription plans (if applicable) can be canceled at any time; access continues until the end of the billing cycle.

## Certification & Exams
- Certificates are issued **within 24 hours** of passing the final exam.
- A minimum score of **80%** is required to pass.
- Learners receive **up to 3 attempts** per exam; additional attempts require support approval.
- Exam retakes must be scheduled at least **24 hours** after a failed attempt.

## Access & Availability
- Course access is **lifetime** for the original purchaser.
- Access includes all updates released for the purchased course.
- Downloadable resources are for **personal use only** and may not be redistributed.

## Enrollment, Transfers & Account Security
- Enrollments are **non-transferable** between users.
- Account sharing is **not permitted**.
- Users are responsible for maintaining secure login credentials.
- If unauthorized access is suspected, users should contact support immediately.

## Support & Response Times
- Support is available **Monday–Friday, 9 AM–5 PM** (local business hours).
- Typical response time is within **1 business day**.
- Urgent issues (billing errors, access loss) are prioritized for same-day review when possible.

## Content Updates & Changes
- Course content may be updated periodically to reflect new tools and best practices.
- Pricing and promotions may change, but enrolled students retain access under their original terms.
- Major curriculum changes may be announced via email or in-platform notifications.

## Acceptable Use
- Content is provided for educational purposes and may not be used to violate laws or regulations.
- Any abusive or fraudulent behavior may result in account suspension.

## Privacy & Data
- Personal data is handled in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
- Progress data may be used to improve course outcomes and platform performance.

## Contact
- Email: support@ailearningcenter.example
- Response times may vary during holidays and scheduled maintenance.
